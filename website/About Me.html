All About Me, Kyle Kariores

Hello, my name is Kyle Kariores. I am currently a freshman at The University of Maine. Althought I go to UMaine, I was not raised in Maine. I grew up in Gloucester, Massachusetts. Growing up I was a big sports, and family guy. Throughout my life I played Baseball, Basketball, Football, and Soccer. Sports were always one of the things my life revolved around as a kid, and I don't think I will ever lose my passion for both playing sports, and watching them. I am a die hard New england sports fan, and want to get season tickets for the Patriots when I grow up. But back to family, I grew up one of seven. I have three siters and three brothers. Since I have so many siblings, it kind of forced me to be a big family guy, and I am glad it did.
